Our Story

Talent Match Africa was born out of an earlier incarnation: InternMatch, founded by Gerard Holland, now the co-founder of TMA.

Talent is equally distributed, opportunity is not.

This powerful truth resonates deeply with us. It captures both the untapped potential of skilled African professionals waiting to be discovered globally and the transformative impact they can have on businesses worldwide seeking to scale and thrive.

Our Journey

2016

The Beginning

Our founder, Gerard Holland, witnessed the employment struggles of international students in Australia. To address this challenge, he established InternMatch, collaborating with universities and education partners to place Australian graduates into valuable internships.

2020

Pivotal Conversation

A significant opportunity emerged through a conversation with Zondwa Mandela. This dialogue shifted Gerard's focus to Africa, raising a compelling question: Why was South Africa home to five universities ranked in the world's top 1% so overlooked as a source of exceptional talent?

2022

African Expansion

Post-pandemic, Gerard made his first visit to Africa and launched The Global Employment Challenge in South Africa. The mission was clear: create sustainable job opportunities for the abundance of African talent.

Present

Talent Match Africa

With insufficient local opportunities for graduates from prestigious universities, and traditional offshore sourcing models needing innovation, we developed a forward thinking approach to international recruitment. The global shift towards remote work became our catalyst to introduce African talent into global workflows.

Our Mission & Approach

Transforming Connections

At Talent Match Africa, our mission is to create transformative connections between exceptional African talent and forward-thinking international organisations. We bridge geographic and cultural boundaries to unlock mutual prosperity.

Empowering Professionals

For African professionals, we provide life-changing career opportunities that recognise their skills, expertise, and potential ensuring fair compensation, professional growth, and global visibility.

Driving Business Growth

For international organisations, we deliver access to a rich, diverse talent pool that brings fresh perspectives, specialised skills, and cost-effective solutions to drive innovation and competitive advantage.

Long-Term Employment

Our mission is to create pathways to long-term employment, career development, and stable income to empower our talented workers. Companies benefit economically from retaining valuable staff.

Ethical Employment & Equity

We ensure ethical talent sourcing, including equal opportunities and access to quality workplaces for our African talent.

Challenging Misconceptions

We aim to fix misconceptions about Africa's talent and infrastructure, showcasing that African professionals are as skilled, capable, and driven as their global counterparts.

Our Values

Embrace Change

We foster open-mindedness, curiosity, and receptiveness to feedback. We encourage bold approaches while supporting colleagues through transformation.

Build Understanding

We promote cross-cultural respect through authentic communication and attentive listening. We share knowledge openly to bridge divides and challenge preconceptions.

Grow Experience

We drive collaboration for exceptional outcomes. Our growth mindset unlocks potential in both individuals and organisations through continuous improvement and international networking.

Create Impact

We own our commitments with high performance standards. Our purpose drives us to deliver meaningful results that make a difference.

Why we do what we do

We’re not about charity, we’re about justice. We believe in solving business challenges in impactful ways while creating employment opportunities for African talent, who are highly skilled, highly qualified, and highly professional.

The TMA Difference

We do not simply take your money, fill your role, and move on. Ours is an innovative outcome-focused talent model, built on sustainable long-term relationships. We want to facilitate ongoing client satisfaction and talent job satisfaction – and witness the positive impact on the world. Our African focus sets us apart as well. tma is committed to enhancing the local economy, celebrating Africa’s diverse skills, deep expertise and contribution to global success.
